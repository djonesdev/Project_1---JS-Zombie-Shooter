Intructions

  • Welcome to Zombie Survival! The worst thing imaginable has happened, a zombie apocolypse! On a supply run your group has been attacked on your way back and you must hold them back before they overrun your camp!
  • Shooting - Simply click to fire your weapon! Watch where you fire though, ammo is in short supply and misses may cost you precious lives! Also beware that some zombies may require more than one shot to put down for good!
  • Scoring - Each regular zombie is worth 10 points, try and get as many points as you can!
  • That's it, fire away and protect your camp!
